Welcome to NSBM Green University

NSBM Green University is the first ever green university in South Asia, open to both national and international students. Established under the Ministry of Education, NSBM offers nationally and internationally recognised, UGC-approved degree programmes across four world-class faculties.

The 26-acre campus is home to more than 12,000 students, supported by highly qualified local and foreign lecturers committed to preparing graduates for the challenges of a rapidly changing world.
