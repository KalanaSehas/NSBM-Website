Who We Are

NSBM Green University is the first ever green university in South Asia and sets an example for the region by paving the way for environmental sustainability. The university is open for both national and international student communities, and it has turned a new chapter in Sri Lankan higher education.

Established under the Ministry of Education, NSBM is renowned for its world-class academic offerings. This state-of-the-art university offers nationally and internationally recognised, UGC-approved degree programmes in four faculties: Business, Computing, Engineering, Science, and Postgraduate Studies.

The university is spread over an area of 26 acres. Currently, more than 12,000 students are studying at the university, guided by highly qualified local and foreign lecturers.

Inspired by the vision of making Sri Lanka the best educational hub in Asia, NSBM Green University is dedicated to gifting future leaders to the world.
