World-Class Facilities

State-of-the-art infrastructure designed to support every dimension of student life.

Modern Lecture Halls
01

Modern Lecture Halls

NSBM features state-of-the-art lecture theatres equipped with the latest audio-visual technology, ergonomic seating, and climate control systems — providing the ideal environment for focused academic learning.

  • Advanced audio-visual systems
  • High-speed Wi-Fi connectivity
  • Ergonomic seating arrangements
Shuttle Service
02

Shuttle Service

Our comprehensive shuttle service connects students from key locations in and around Colombo to the university campus, with multiple routes, fixed schedules, and comfortable air-conditioned buses.

  • Multiple routes covering Colombo
  • Fixed, reliable timetable
  • Air-conditioned fleet
Sports Complex
03

Sports Complex

The NSBM Sports Complex is one of the finest in Sri Lanka, featuring a full-sized cricket ground, football pitches, basketball courts, a swimming pool, and indoor facilities for badminton and table tennis.

  • Cricket ground and football pitches
  • Basketball and badminton courts
  • Swimming pool
Modern Auditorium
04

Modern Auditorium

The university's grand auditorium hosts convocations, cultural performances, large-scale workshops, and guest lectures, equipped with professional sound and lighting systems.

  • Professional sound system
  • Stage lighting and AV setup
  • Large seating capacity
Hostel Facilities
05

Hostel Facilities

Comfortable, secure, and well-maintained hostel accommodation is available for both local and international students, with furnished rooms and essential amenities fostering a vibrant community spirit.

  • Furnished rooms with amenities
  • 24/7 security
  • Cafeteria access
Library Facilities
06

Library Facilities

The NSBM library houses an extensive collection of academic books, journals, e-resources, and research databases, with dedicated study rooms, quiet reading zones, and fast internet access.

  • Extensive physical and digital collection
  • Private study rooms
  • 24/7 online database access
