Faculty of Computing

The Faculty of Computing offers top-tier education in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Data Science, Cybersecurity, and Artificial Intelligence. Students benefit from state-of-the-art computer labs and close ties with leading technology companies locally and internationally.

BSc Computer Science BSc Software Engineering BSc Data Science BSc Cybersecurity BSc AI and Machine Learning
Faculty of Business
Business

Faculty of Business

The Faculty of Business at NSBM is a hub for future entrepreneurs, leaders, and innovators. It offers internationally recognised degrees and maintains strong industry connections, providing students with internship opportunities and live project exposure.

BBM Business Management BSc Accounting and Finance BSc Marketing BSc HRM BSc International Business
Faculty of Engineering
Engineering

Faculty of Engineering

Dedicated to innovation and hands-on learning, the Faculty of Engineering prepares students for careers in Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, and Electronic Engineering with world-class laboratories and industry visit programmes.

BEng Civil Engineering BEng Electrical Engineering BEng Mechanical Engineering BEng Electronic and Telecom

Faculty of Science

The Faculty of Science fosters critical scientific thinking and research excellence. Students have access to advanced research laboratories and work closely with faculty members who are active researchers in their fields.

BSc Bioinformatics BSc Food Science and Technology BSc Quantity Surveying BSc Physical Science
